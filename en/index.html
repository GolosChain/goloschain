About us

Golos.io was launched on October 18, 2016, as the first decentralized, blockchain-based social media platform designed specifically for the Russian-speaking community. Built as a fork of the Steem blockchain, it leveraged the Graphene 2.0 codebase and operated on a Delegated Proof-of-Stake (DPoS) consensus mechanism.

In 2019, Golos.io migrated to the CyberWay blockchain, enhancing performance and scalability to better serve its growing user base.
